Dr, Evan R. Collins, President of the State University College of Education,
Albany, will speak at thedinner meeting of the Albany College of Education
Library Aluani Association on Thursday evening, October 12, This dinner will
celebrate the 355th anniversary of the founding of the Department of Librarianship,
and will be held in conjunction with the New York Library Association Conference
at the Laurels Hotel and Country Club, Monticello, New York.

Dr. Irving Verechoor, new director of the Depertment of Librarianship, and
Professor Robert Burgess, who recently returned from two and one-half years in
Korea, will greet the Alumni. Guests of honor will be Miss Georgiana Maar,
President elect of the New York Library Association, and Mrs. Augusta Baker,
coordinator of Children’s Services, New York Public Library. Both Miss Maar
and Mre. Beker are graduates of the Department of Librarianship at Albany.

Mr. Robert Chatterton, Librarian of the Greenville Central School is the

Treasurer of this Alumni Association.
