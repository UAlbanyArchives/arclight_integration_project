OFFICIAL STATEMENT REGARDING UPCOMING “LIVE FREE TOUR”
AT UNIVERSITY AT ALBANY FEATURING QUEERPHOBIC
COMMENTATOR CANDACE OWENS

From its very eeation, Queer Jay UA has aimed to prioritize the safety and well being ofthe
Queer and Trans population on campus. It comes to us with disturbance but ultimately as no
surprise that UAIbany has rented out the CC Ballroom to Turing Point USA for their ‘Live Free
‘Tour’ featuring Candace Owens, who has built her eareer around racist and queerphobie rhetoric,
Queer Joy UA wholeheartedly denounces this event.

Queer Joy UA urges caution to our Queer and ‘Trans siblings, and especially students of
color, who may be inclined to protest this event. While we standin solidarity with anyone who
does choose to attend this event in protest, we urge our community to take care of ourselves first
and foremost and evaluate the risks that attending this event may pose. Given that UAIbany
failed to stand with marginalized students as their images were being blasted on social media and
used in right-wing news outlets in the wake of student protests last semester, leading to violent
threats and harassment, and given how high profile Candace Owens is as a public figure, we urge
students to take care of themselves, and to consider attending alternative actions to avoid putting
themselves in a traumatic position, Turning Point USA notes that the ‘audience will be video
recorded” and that “video and audio surveillance will bein use,” and this compounded withthe
personal and identifiable information they are requiring attendees to provide will lead to
harassment from Turning Point USA's national hateful base.
